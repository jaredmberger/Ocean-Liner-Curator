Authentic Titanic Postcards and Promotional Material

Titanic postcard material is more varied than many collectors first expect. Some cards are memorial pieces issued after the sinking. Others are promotional images tied to White Star presentation and passenger publicity. A smaller group survives mainly through archives, institutional collections, or collecting references rather than easy, publication-safe scans.
