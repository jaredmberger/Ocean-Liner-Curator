Submit Ocean Liner Art

Ocean Liner Curator occasionally reviews and features original artwork that engages thoughtfully with ocean liners—design, history, and material culture. Submissions are reviewed case-by-case. Not all submissions can be published.

⁂ Quick expectation: This is a curatorial review process, not an open gallery. Selected works may be featured with full credit, and may include a short curator’s note for context.

What This Page Is For

Use this page to submit original ocean-liner-related artwork for consideration. If your work is selected, it may appear in a curated “Selected Reader Artwork” section, with attribution and a stable permalink.

What I’m Looking For

  • Original work inspired by ocean liners (historic or interpretive).
  • Historically informed depictions, design studies, posters, technical drawings, or concept work.
  • Clear labeling when something is interpretive rather than a reconstruction.
  • Thoughtful context (brief statement, what ship/era inspired it, what sources you referenced if relevant).

What I Don’t Accept

  • Uncredited derivative work (traces/edits of other artists’ work).
  • Unlicensed commercial imagery (studio stills, film frames, copyrighted marketing art) presented as original.
  • Content that relies on sensational or unsupported claims presented as fact.
  • Low-resolution images that cannot be displayed cleanly on the site.
AI note: If you used AI tools in any part of the workflow, disclose it in the submission. I may still decline AI-generated imagery depending on fit and documentation. Transparency is required either way.

Submission Checklist

Files
JPG or PNG preferred. Aim for 2000px wide minimum for featured images (larger is welcome).
Credits
Name / artist handle, optional website or social link, and preferred credit line.
Statement
150–300 words describing the work, subject, and intent. Sources optional but appreciated.
Rights
You must own the work and grant permission for Ocean Liner Curator to display it (non-exclusive).

Review & Publication

  • Submissions are reviewed periodically. If selected, I’ll contact you using the email provided.
  • I may request minor edits (cropping, caption clarity, or historical labeling) before publication.
  • Publication is editorially curated. I may decline submissions without detailed feedback.

Rights & Permission Summary

If your submission is selected, you grant Ocean Liner Curator a non-exclusive permission to publish and display your work on this site and (optionally) promote it on Ocean Liner Curator social channels, with full credit. You retain ownership and may publish the work elsewhere.

Removal: Ocean Liner Curator reserves the right to remove published submissions (for example: rights concerns, misattribution, or policy changes). If you need a piece removed, contact me and I’ll handle it promptly.
